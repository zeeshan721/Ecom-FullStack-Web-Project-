How It All Started

A Dream Stitched
with Passion

LUXE was born in 2020 in Lahore, Pakistan — out of a simple frustration. Why did Pakistanis have to settle for either low quality or sky-high prices? Why couldn't fashion, shoes and cosmetics all be found in one beautiful, trustworthy place?

We started with a small collection of 30 clothing pieces and a vision to redefine Pakistani e-commerce. Today, LUXE carries over 650 products across clothing, footwear and cosmetics — delivering to 100+ cities across Pakistan.

Every product we carry is hand-selected by our team. We test everything before it goes live. Because you deserve the best — and we won't settle for anything less.

Ayesha Rahman Founder & CEO, LUXE
